Project Description

ASA City is a futuristic, sustainable, and human-centric urban development concept. The design integrates mixed-use development, efficient transportation networks, green spaces, and smart infrastructure to create a livable and resilient city.

The project explores how modern cities can balance rapid growth with environmental responsibility. Our team has worked on zoning strategies, street layouts, public amenities, and digital systems that support both citizens and administrators.

A key emphasis of ASA City is the use of data structures and algorithms as planning tools. Road networks are modeled as weighted graphs, zoning mimics hierarchical tree structures, and urban services rely on efficient searching and optimization. This demonstrates that modern city planning is fundamentally a data-driven process.

Overall, ASA City represents a complete city concept that combines planning theory, teamwork, and real-world application. It illustrates how urban development can be enhanced through analytics, structured data, and intelligent systems to create a resilient, connected, and livable city.

Course Overview

About the Course

ASA City is developed as part of the Advanced City Development course. The course integrates principles of urban planning, architectural design, transportation engineering, sustainability, and smart city technologies.

Through studio sessions, case studies, and collaborative design tasks, students transform conceptual planning ideas into detailed city layouts, focusing on practicality, functionality, and innovation.

What You Will Learn

  • Fundamentals of urban planning and city structure
  • Designing road networks, blocks, and land-use zoning
  • Integrating public transport and walkable public spaces
  • Applying sustainability principles in city development
  • Using technology to create smart and connected cities
  • Working in teams to plan and present a complete city concept
