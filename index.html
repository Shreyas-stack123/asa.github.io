Urban Design & Smart City Studio Project

Project Description

This city-building project focuses on designing a futuristic, sustainable, and human-centric city. Our concept integrates mixed-use development, efficient transportation networks, green spaces, and smart infrastructure to create a livable and resilient urban environment.

The project explores how modern cities can balance rapid growth with environmental responsibility. We have worked on zoning strategies, street layouts, public amenities, and digital systems that support both citizens and administrators. Course Overview

About the Course

This project has been developed as part of the Advanced City Development course. The course introduces students to core principles of urban planning, architectural design, transportation systems, sustainability, and smart city technologies.

Through a combination of studio work, case studies, and project-based learning, students learn how to convert conceptual ideas into practical city layouts and development plans. The emphasis is on problem-solving, teamwork, and real-world application of planning tools and techniques.

What You Will Learn

  • Fundamentals of urban planning and city structure
  • Designing road networks, blocks, and land-use zoning
  • Integrating public transport and pedestrian-friendly spaces
  • Applying sustainability principles in city development
  • Using technology to create smart and connected cities
  • Working in teams to plan and present a complete city concept
